Official Release

13. 08. 2013

Geometry Dash is released on Android on 13 August 2013, later on IOS. This game was not well known at the time and didn't have much features.

Main Levels:

  • Stereo Madness
  • Back On Track
  • Polargeist
  • Dry Out
  • Base After Base
  • Can't Let Go
  • Jumper

Features:

  • Ship Gamemode.
  • 13 customizable characters with 12 different colors.
  • Level Editor with around 36 objects.
  • User made levels you could play and like.
